Welcome to Nexaero Protocols.

This is not a game for pilots. It’s for those who keep them alive.
You are an MCT — Maintenance Control Technician — responsible for fault diagnostics, emergency repairs, component calibration, and system release approval. Your office? A hangar lit by flickering sodium lamps. Your tools? A checklist, a scanner, your instinct, and not enough time.
Every plane cleared or grounded — is your decision.
Every oversight — your weight to carry.
