Tech Room Notes

  • Is Nexaero a flight simulator?

    No. It’s a maintenance simulator — entirely ground-based, pre-flight focused.

  • Can I crash a plane?

    You won’t see it crash. But your missed decisions will be logged — and reflected in post-flight reports.

  • Is it real-time?

    Yes. A shift is time-compressed but simulated live. You’ll feel the pressure.

  • Is this game based on real procedures?

    Yes — adapted from FAA and EASA standards, simplified for play but accurate in principle.

  • Do I need aviation knowledge to play?

    No. You’ll learn through gameplay. But attention to detail is everything.

  • Can I replay missions?

    Yes — but the system will randomize diagnostics and fault patterns to test adaptability.

  • Is there multiplayer?

    Not yet. But team-based “hangar mode” is in development.

  • What platforms?

    PC first. Console ports possible post-launch.

  • What genre is this, really?

    A mix of simulator, decision-making drama, and logic-driven strategy. Think Papers Please meets Airport Madness, but in a dark hangar.
